Ghanaian Recipes

1. Jollof Rice

A flavorful one-pot rice dish cooked in a tomato-based sauce with spices and vegetables.

2. Banku and Tilapia

A traditional Ghanaian dish made with fermented corn and cassava dough served with grilled tilapia fish.

3. Fufu with Groundnut Soup

Fufu, a staple food in Ghana, made from pounded cassava and plantain, served with rich groundnut soup.

4. Red Red (Black Eyed Peas Stew)

A popular Ghanaian dish made with black-eyed peas cooked in a flavorful tomato stew served with fried plantains.

5. Kelewele

Spicy fried plantains seasoned with ginger, cloves, and pepper, a popular street food in Ghana.

6. Waakye

A savory Ghanaian dish made with rice and beans cooked in a spicy tomato stew, served with assorted sides.

7. Groundnut Soup with Rice Balls

A hearty soup made with groundnuts, vegetables, and meat served with rice balls.

8. Grilled Chicken with Shito

Marinated chicken grilled to perfection and served with spicy Ghanaian black pepper sauce (shito).

9. Kenkey with Fish

Steamed cornmeal served with fried fish and pepper sauce, a popular dish in Ghana.

10. Omo Tuo with Peanut Soup

Rice balls served with a rich and creamy peanut soup, a comforting meal loved by many Ghanaians.

Foreign Recipes

1. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Tender chicken breasts cooked in a creamy garlic parmesan sauce.

2. Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Succulent shrimp cooked with garlic, butter, lemon juice, and white wine served over pasta.

3. Filet Mignon with Red Wine Sauce

Juicy filet mignon steaks seared to perfection and topped with a rich red wine sauce.

4. Lobster Thermidor

Lobster meat cooked in a creamy cheese sauce and baked until golden and bubbly.

5. Beef Wellington

Tender beef fillet wrapped in puff pastry with mushroom duxelles and prosciutto.

6. Caprese Stuffed Chicken

Chicken breasts stuffed with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil, then baked to perfection.

7. Pesto Shrimp Linguine

Shrimp tossed in homemade pesto sauce and served over linguine pasta.

8. Vegetable Risotto

Creamy risotto made with Arborio rice, mixed vegetables, and Parmesan cheese.

9. Coq au Vin

Classic French dish of chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon.

10. Salmon with Dill Sauce

Pan-seared salmon fillets topped with a creamy dill sauce.
